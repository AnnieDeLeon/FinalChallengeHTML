👋 Hi! We are Codédex, a brand new learn to code platform for high schoolers and college students.

We are beyond excited to announce that we just launched! 🥳🚀🎉

15:39 PM - 28 Jul 2022

After working at the intersection of education and technology for the past seven years, I found that project-based learning and gamification are the most effective ways to keep young students engaged in their coding journey.

Mix that in with a touch of 8-bit aesthetics, and we aim to create nothing short of a magical experience. 👾

This v1.0 launch includes:

  • 📱 Mobile view
  • 🔓 Challenge unlock
  • 🏃‍♂️ User progress
  • ⏳ User streak
  • 📛 Badges
  • 🛠 Project tutorials
  • 🏗 React & Next.js

Please check out Codédex and let me know what you think! 🙏
